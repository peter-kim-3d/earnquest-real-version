## üéØ General Guidelines for All Child Avatars

**DO:**
- Use bright, saturated, cheerful colors
- Keep designs cute and friendly (not scary)
- Make eyes large and friendly (appeals to children)
- Use simple geometric shapes (easy to recognize)
- Create soft, rounded features (approachable)
- Ensure they work at small sizes (40x40px)
- Make each character clearly distinguishable
- Keep expressions happy and positive

**DON'T:**
- Make them too detailed or complex
- Use realistic/photographic animal features
- Create scary or aggressive expressions
- Include sharp/pointy elements that look dangerous
- Use dark or muted colors
- Make them too childish (remember: ages 5-14)
- Include text or letters
- Use culturally specific symbols

---

## üìè Technical Requirements

1. **Resolution:** 512x512px minimum (can scale up for high-DPI displays)
2. **Format:** PNG-24 with alpha channel (transparent background)
3. **Color Profile:** sRGB
4. **File Size:** Under 100KB each (optimized)
5. **Naming Convention:** `child-avatar-[number].png` (e.g., `child-avatar-1.png`)
